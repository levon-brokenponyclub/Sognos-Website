## Understanding Genogram in care

A genogram is a visual map of family relationships and support networks used across health and social care.

It gives practitioners a clear view of how people are connected, where support sits, and patterns that may influence care, safety, and outcomes.

Widely used in child protection, foster care, mental health, and community services, it helps build a more complete picture of a person's situation.

**SognosGenogram** takes this further — from automated creation to AI-supported insights — helping practitioners surface patterns, strengthen understanding, and make more informed care planning decisions.

## Understanding SognosGenogram — FAQs

**What is the difference between a genogram and a family tree?**
A family tree shows basic relationships. A genogram goes further by mapping roles, dynamics and patterns that can impact care and decision-making.

**When should you use genogram software?**
When you need to understand complex family or support networks, identify risks and plan care across multiple people involved.

**How does SognosGenogram fit with case management systems?**
It adds a clear visual layer of relationships, helping teams understand context alongside client records.

**How does SognosGenogram help frontline teams?**
It shows who is involved at a glance, reduces time spent searching through notes, and supports faster, more confident decisions.

**Can SognosGenogram help identify risk?**
Yes. Mapping relationships makes it easier for practitioners to spot gaps, pressure points and dynamics that may impact safety or wellbeing.

**Who benefits from using SognosGenogram?**
Case workers, support coordinators, clinicians and service managers across child protection, foster care, mental health, disability and community services.

**How is SognosGenogram different from standard CRM relationship views?**
It provides a structured visual map of family and support networks, making complex connections easier to understand at a glance.

**How does SognosGenogram fit with SognosCare?**
It extends SognosCare by adding a clear visual view of relationships and support networks alongside client and case records.

**How is the SognosGenogram used in Child and Family Services?**
In SognosCare Child and Family Services, the Genogram sits at the centre of every case, giving practitioners and frontline care teams a clear, visual view of family relationships and context.
