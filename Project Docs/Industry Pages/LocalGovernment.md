# Local Government

Source: https://sognos.com.au/industries/local-government/

## Resilient and Connected Communities Through Managed Infrastructure, Facilities and Services.

The local council/public sector has some of the most interesting requirements when it comes to managing the way work is executed in the field. From utility installations to community-based health, service expectations are high, and budgetary pressure is even higher. As a result, today's civil servants are overworked and under-utilised.

Workers are often handling a number of variables before, during, and after completing required work orders. In order to be efficient, they need easy access to information and community data, as well as the ability to collect information and submit reports on site.

## Maximise Your Budget Through Accountability.

Take advantage of FSM to enhance efficiency, responsiveness, and service delivery to your community.

- **Maintenance and Repairs** — Maintain and repair public infrastructure such as roads, bridges, parks, and utilities by equipped teams with relevant devices and tools, to ensure timely inspections, repairs, and upkeep of assets.
