## Residential & Aged Care

### Deliver Safe and Quality Care with Services Based on Needs, Goals and Preferences.

With an ageing population and one of the world's highest life expectancies, there is mounting pressure to provide superior service to Australia's older generations. Sognos offers a built-for-purpose field management solution, that focuses on ease of managing care recipient profiles, including onboarding and care planning, budgeting, through to service delivery, scheduling, and invoicing.

Our service delivery approach allows you to:
- Monitor and predict participant behaviour
- Support the delivery of health services in-office and remotely
- Ensure your customers a stress free experience

### Review Information Safely and Securely in Real-Time.

**Home Care Services** — In-home care services where caregivers visit clients who are elderly in their homes to help with daily activities.

**Health Monitoring & Assessment** — When dispatched to conduct health assessments, monitor vital signs, or provide medical services such as administering injections or wound care to clients who are elderly, in their homes.

**Emergency Response** — Have all relevant information when called upon to provide emergency response services in the event of medical emergencies or accidents.

**Medical Equipment Installation & Maintenance** — Install, maintain, and repair this equipment in clients' homes.

**Transportation Services** — Route management and monitoring of transportation services.

**Environmental Assessments** — Assessments of clients' homes to identify potential hazards or barriers to mobility and provide recommendations.

**Client Support & Education** — Field service personnel may also provide education and support to clients who are elderly and their families.

## NDIS

### Connecting Providers, Support Workers, Participants, and Families.

As a NDIS Provider organisation, FSM delivers real-time access to scheduling and rostering, incident reporting, document provision, and regulatory compliance. We offer your team visibility into participants' plans, service bookings, budgets, and support.

- Participant NDIS plan and delivery management
- Integrated service booking
- Budget planning, management, and monitoring
- Easily scaleable to your requirements

### Empower Your People to Provide The Best Quality Care.

**Planning with Effectiveness** — The link between care provider and participant in real-time allows for immediate support or adjustments to plans as needed, leading to more effective and positive outcomes.

**Reduced Admin Burden** — Submitting reports and actions while on site ensures those delivering on site services streamline the process and administrative burden enabling more time to be spent on direct participant support.

**Community Integration** — FSM can support participants in accessing community resources and participating in social activities, promoting inclusion and integration within their communities.

**Crisis Response & Prevention** — Having the right information when you need it allows field/remote workers to quickly respond to crises or changes in a participants' circumstances, helping to prevent escalation and ensuring timely support.

**Data Security & Compliance** — Valuable data and insights can inform program improvements and organisation policy decisions. Ensuring you have the right regulatory compliance and security to protect the information is critical.

Overall, integrating field service into your organization's framework can lead to more efficient, person-led support delivery and improved outcomes through providing support and services to people with disabilities, their families, and carers.

## Social Care

### Ensure Clients Build Relationships, Independence, and Enjoy Life.

Like the rest of the healthcare industry those who are immersed in Social Care are constantly challenged by the need to manage a complex array of variables. With Sognos FSM solution you can create in-depth client profiles and case notes to build rapport, set goals, and track and share progress — all while on the move. Client notes and files can be easily accessed, added, and updated directly so progress can be tracked as needed.

- Client profile management, goals, progress notes, care plans & more
- Notes and records of client preferences and interactions to assist care givers in making decisions
- Team management to ensure your people are working with productivity

### Exceptional Client Care is the Goal. Delivered with Readily Accessible and Accurate Information, Powered by FSM.

**Optimised Scheduling & Routing** — Efficiently set up routes to minimise travel time, reduce costs, and ensuring that social care workers spend more time providing support to individuals in need.

**Anytime, Anywhere Communication** — Facilitate real-time communication between social care workers and their supervisors or support staff for quick decision-making, problem-solving, and the ability to adapt to changing circumstances promptly.

**Secure Data Collection & Reporting** — Collect data in the field, safely and securely including assessments, observations, and client interactions to be shared in a central management system, leading to more accurate and timely reporting.

The right FSM solution gives your organisation the improved visibility your workers require to be productive while focusing on providing the best quality of care to your client.
