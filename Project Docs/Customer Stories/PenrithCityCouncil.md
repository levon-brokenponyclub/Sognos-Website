# Penrith City Council

Source: https://sognos.com.au/customer-stories/penrith-city-council-case-study/

**Industry:** City Council — Public Sector
**State:** New South Wales, Australia
**Size:** 300 Technicians
**Products and Services:** Dynamics 365 Field Service

---

Deloitte Digital subcontracted Sognos to provide Penrith City Council with an entirely custom solution to manage PCC's day-to-day Resource Crew (team member) movements for their Field Operations team, given Sognos's background and expertise with Dynamics 365 Field Service.

Sognos provided PCC with a custom-built solution that automatically manages all the background operations of managing and juggling jobs between teams when team members are moved across different teams. The coordinators who had completely refused to use the Field Service solution due to time-consuming manual operations now love the 3-click solution. With this, they can manage their movements within 10 seconds — a task that earlier took at least 25 minutes per movement and still didn't guarantee accurate data for time sheets.

## The Situation

- Manual process to move team members from one team to another
- Manual process to create/cancel jobs for newly added team members between teams
- This added very unproductive time for the coordinators
- Coordinators found it hard to keep track of movements, jobs, and timesheets
- No tracking of jobs led to incorrect time entries and time sheets
- End-to-end chaos between field techs, coordinators, and back-office team

## The Solution

- Custom workflows implemented to automatically manage jobs when team members are moved across different teams
- Custom 3-button-based solution for coordinators to record movements
- Custom Day Sheet Report provided for coordinators to track, manage, and have visibility of their day-to-day operations

## The Impact

- Coordinators agreed to use the solution which was earlier protested against
- Correct time sheet and time entries generated because of the right background workflow automation implemented
- Drastic increase in the productivity of coordinators as the automated workflows removed all need for manual operations
