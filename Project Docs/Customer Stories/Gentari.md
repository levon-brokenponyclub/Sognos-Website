# Gentari Solar Australia

Source: https://sognos.com.au/customer-stories/gentari-case-study/

*Formerly known as Wirsol Energy*

**Industry:** Solar Farm Maintenance (Renewable Energy) / Utilities
**State:** Operates Nationally
**Size:** 30–50
**Products and Services:** Dynamics 365 Field Service, Dynamics 365 Business Central, Office 365

---

Gentari Solar Australia, formerly known as Wirsol Energy, is a clean energy leader focused on accelerating the transition to net zero. Across Australia, Gentari owns and operates large-scale solar farms, managing over 10,000 individual assets (solar components) spread across multiple sites. With a mix of in-house technicians and subcontractor teams, Gentari required a modern solution to track and manage preventive maintenance, streamline workflows, and provide real-time visibility into asset performance.

## The Challenge

Managing thousands of components across remote solar farms meant Gentari needed:

- Full asset history visibility for each component
- Automated maintenance scheduling to reduce manual admin
- Mobile-friendly tools for technicians in the field
- A single platform to replace manual processes and disconnected systems

Gentari's priority was to empower technicians to make data-driven decisions on-site, capture accurate maintenance data, and reduce downtime.

## The Solution

Partnering with Sognos, Gentari implemented Microsoft Dynamics 365 Field Service, delivering a connected, end-to-end platform that:

- Centralises asset records and service histories
- Automates preventive maintenance routines
- Enables real-time data capture via mobile devices
- Improves scheduling efficiency and operational oversight

The intuitive platform ensures technicians have all the information they need in the field — boosting productivity and extending asset lifespan.

## The Results

Since going live, Gentari has seen:

- Improved efficiency through automation of routine tasks
- Higher technician satisfaction, with easy-to-use mobile tools
- Complete operational visibility from asset to service record
- Better decision-making with data captured in real time

Over a year into using Dynamics 365 Field Service, adoption rates remain high, with positive feedback from both field teams and management.

## Looking Ahead

With Sognos as their strategic technology partner, Gentari continues to expand the capabilities of their Dynamics 365 platform, replacing legacy systems, improving workflows, and unlocking new opportunities for innovation in renewable energy operations.
