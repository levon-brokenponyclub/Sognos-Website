Flourish Australia is a leading not-for-profit organisation supporting people with lived experience of mental health issues to live fulfilling, independent lives. With more than 1,100 staff across Australia, Flourish delivers a wide range of community-based services, employment support, and recovery-focused programs.

## Transforming Service Operations with Microsoft Dynamics 365

To replace its ageing Client Information Management System (CIMS), Flourish Australia partnered with Sognos to design and implement a modern, cloud-based solution built on the Microsoft Dynamics 365 and Power Platform. The new system – known internally as *Flourish Connect* – serves as the central operational platform for the organisation, consolidating multiple business functions into a single source of truth.

Flourish Connect manages everything from support operations and stakeholder engagement to participant self-service, marketing, and feedback management – delivering seamless visibility tailored to each user's role and context.

## The Situation

- Legacy CIMS system reaching end of life and no longer meeting business needs
- Double handling of data and manual processes creating significant administrative overhead
- Non-standard processes and poor data quality impacting funder reporting and participant support
- Multiple siloed systems managing critical operational and service-delivery functions

## The Solution

Sognos implemented a highly configurable Dynamics 365 solution designed specifically for the complex needs of the mental health and community services sector. Key features include:

- Configurable modules for Enquiry, Intake, Support Delivery, Billing, and Claiming (integrated with the NDIS PRODA Portal)
- Custom Program Profiles and Assessment Tools to support program flexibility and future growth
- Advanced reporting using SSRS and Power BI for accurate Funder and stakeholder insights
- Seamless integration with T1-HR, T1-Finance, Emplive Payroll, and Rostering systems
- Stakeholder and Participant Insights dashboards (Sales and Continuous Improvement)

## The Impact

- Supporting over 1,100 users across the Flourish Australia network
- Providing a single source of truth for all operational and reporting needs
- Enabling the team to eliminate redundant manual steps and improve productivity
- Offering high configurability to adapt and extend the solution as organisational needs evolve

## Looking Ahead

With Dynamics 365 and Power Platform at its core, Flourish Australia now has a unified digital foundation to support innovation, continuous improvement, and sustainable growth – ensuring technology keeps pace with its mission to support mental health recovery across Australia.
