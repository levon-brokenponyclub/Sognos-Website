**Industry:** Transport

Auckland Airport is New Zealand's largest and busiest airport, serving as a critical gateway for international and domestic travel and freight. As a key piece of national infrastructure, the airport is committed to innovation and operational excellence to meet the evolving needs of passengers, airlines, and stakeholders.

## Expanding Digital Capabilities with Microsoft Dynamics 365 and Power Platform

Following the successful implementation of a new Dynamics 365-based solution, Auckland Airport (AKL) gained the confidence and visibility needed to consider the platform a strategic asset. The results highlighted the value of investing in a scalable, integrated system that could support ongoing transformation.

With Sognos now serving as AKL's strategic Microsoft Dynamics 365 partner, the relationship has evolved into one of continuous improvement. Sognos is working closely with the airport team to optimise the current platform and progressively expand its capabilities across the broader Dynamics 365 and Power Platform ecosystem.

This strategic direction includes replacing legacy, non-Microsoft applications with modern, cloud-based tools that deliver better alignment, agility, and data-driven insights, supporting AKL's long-term operational goals.

## The Situation

- System reaching end of life, tight timeline
- Central system for Operations and Engineering Services to manage faults in all engineering assets
- Manual rules and processes for managing and scheduling faults for asset maintenance, from incision to cost recovery and regulatory reporting
- Legacy system siloed from GIS, EAM, Incident Management System, Risk Management System, Finance System, and Emergency Management System

## The Solution

- Custom-built scheduling workflow to automatically assign jobs for asset fault management and maintenance based on fault type, priority, and technician capabilities and skillsets (in-house techs and contractors)
- End-to-end Fault Management solution (based on Dynamics 365 Field Services) to manage faults from logging, fault diagnosis, auto-routing, scheduling, mobile application, cost recovery, and regulatory reporting ensuring compliance requirements such as JSAs and SWMS
- Solution integrated with JDE CAM, IMPAC Risk Management, Incident Management, ArcGIS, and Emergency Management system

## The Impact

- System rolled out within timeline and budget
- 100% business and end-user adaptability of the newly implemented system
- Current solution leveraged by the entire Operations and Engineering Services team to manage all engineering assets on Airside and Landside
- End-to-end integrated system to support end user needs
- Added value for the business to enter a Continuous Improvement mode to invest further on the solution and release new features
